Who is "The Motivational Stranger"?

Founded in 2017, "The Motivational Stranger" was created by Sharon Metzger. Metzger is a Senior Linguistics Major at Salisbury University who has anxiety herself and wants to be a support system for those who may not have one.

"The Motiational Stranger" provides a safe, comforting and funny vibe, so that people can feel comfortable in order to achieve their goals.
